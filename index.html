Tighter Spaces and Traps

Chapter 3's environment is designed to heighten fear and difficulty. Narrow hallways, dark rooms, and strategically placed traps force players to plan movement carefully. While Chapter 2 had open rooms and predictable paths, Chapter 3 keeps players constantly on edge. Quick decisions are required during chases, and small mistakes can be costly.

Increased Resource Management

Managing tools and items is more demanding in Chapter 3. Gas Masks, batteries for tools, and other essential items are limited, and players must decide when to use them strategically. Improper usage can leave you vulnerable in critical moments, a challenge less emphasized in Chapter 2.

More Intensive Stealth and Strategy

Chapter 3 places a stronger emphasis on stealth and planning. Running blindly or attempting to confront enemies directly is rarely a viable option. Players must analyze patrol patterns, listen to audio cues, and choose hiding spots wisely. This makes every encounter more tense and every decision more meaningful.

Psychological Pressure and Horror

Finally, the horror in Chapter 3 is more immersive. Darker lighting, environmental storytelling, and sound design amplify fear. Unlike Chapter 2, which relied more on jump scares, Chapter 3 creates sustained tension, forcing players to stay alert at all times.
